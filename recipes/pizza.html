Pizza

Easy way to make pizza!

This is a great recipe when you don't want to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  3. Lightly grease a pizza pan
  4. Place warm water in a bowl
  5. Add yeast and sugar
  6. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  7. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture
  8. Beat until smooth
  9. Let rest for 5 minutes
  10. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle
  11. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan
  12. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  13. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
